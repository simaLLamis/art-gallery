Our Story

We started with a simple idea: to create a space where traditional and digital art could coexist and converse. From a small pop-up gallery in the arts district, we've grown into a recognized institution dedicated to showcasing both established and emerging artists working across mediums. We believe in the transformative power of art to challenge perspectives, and this drives everything we do.
